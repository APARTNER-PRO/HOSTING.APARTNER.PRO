Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2025

Contents

    1. Data We Collect

    We collect: identity data (name, email, phone); payment data (processed via secure gateways — we do not store card details); technical data (IP, browser, OS, cookies); usage data (logs, load stats).

    2. How We Use Your Data

    Data is used solely to provide the service, process payments, deliver technical support, and prevent fraud. We never sell personal data to third parties for marketing.

    3. Cookies

    We use essential cookies (session, authentication) and optional analytics cookies. You can disable analytics cookies in your browser settings.

    4. Data Security

    Data is stored on secure EU servers with TLS encryption in transit and AES-256 for sensitive data at rest. Access is restricted to employees who need it.

    5. Your Rights (GDPR)

    You have the right to: access, rectify, erase, restrict processing, object to processing, and data portability. Contact: [email protected]

    6. Third Parties

    Data is shared only with trusted technical partners (payment processors, CDN). Each signs a GDPR-compliant data processing agreement.

    7. Retention

    Active account data is retained for the service duration. After closure, data is deleted within 30 days unless legally required otherwise.

    8. Policy Changes

    Material changes are communicated by email at least 30 days in advance.
