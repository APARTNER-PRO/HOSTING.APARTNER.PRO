Refund Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2025

Contents

    1. 30-Day Money-Back Guarantee

    If for any reason you are not satisfied within the first 30 days of your first payment, we refund 100% — no questions asked. Applies to new accounts only, one per customer.

    2. Plan Change Credits

    When upgrading, unused balance is credited toward the new plan. When downgrading, the price difference is credited as account balance for future payments.

    3. Subscription Renewals

    Automatic renewals are non-refundable if the service has been used. Cancel at least 24 hours before the renewal date to avoid unwanted charges.

    4. Non-Refundable Cases

    • Violation of Terms of Service
    • Account suspended for abuse
    • Domain registrations and SSL certificates after issuance
    • Renewal refund requests after the 30-day window

    5. Refund Process

    Email [email protected] with your account email. We process within 1 business day; funds return in 3-7 business days.

    6. Account Credits

    Instead of a card refund you may choose instant account credits usable for any future service.

    7. Disputes

    If you feel a refund was unfairly denied: [email protected]
